AnimalDex



Our Mission Statement

AnimalDex is an interactive web application allowing you access to the most up-to-date geographical and biological information on animals located within national parks. This application will give feedback based on physical description, habitat, population numbers within the searched region and last spotted location. Having this information will allow wildlife observers, educators and recreationalists to find an animal that they are looking for and use that data however they see fit.
